OUR MENU

Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.)

Hamburger

Ground beef, or minced meat mince or other meat used to make hamburgers. Patty, the ground meat in a circular shape to fit the burger bun and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.).

French fries

French fries are served hot, either soft or crispy, and are generally eaten as part of lunch or dinner or by themselves as a snack, and they commonly appear on the menus of diners, fast food restaurants, pubs, and bars.
